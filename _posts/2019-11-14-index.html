If you're a legal professional who wants to do practical stuff with legal technology, this site is designed for you.

This site is designed around video tutorials that show you how to use technology to do stuff that actually matters to legal professionals: automate documents, update your case management system, and generally make your work easier.

You don't need to be an expert.

These tutorials are designed for people with zero programming experience. Not everyone needs to learn how to code to do cool stuff with legal technology.

Sounds great, but I don't have a big budget for tech stuff.

Welcome to the club. Nearly all of the technologies I use are free or open source. You don't need to go spend a bunch of money every month on technology.

Why make these tutorials?

Legal technology has a problem: it sounds cool, but what good is it for the day-to-day operations of a legal professional?
